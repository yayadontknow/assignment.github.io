About Us

Run for bright future

"Run for Kheng Chee" is organized to raise funds for SJKC Kheng Chee. We aim to raise RM20,000 through this event to resolve problems such as the lack of several classrooms and school facilities.

Since the school opened in 2014, the number of students has increased over the years causing the school to have insufficient classrooms. The school also lacks facilities such as a Music room, Computer room, and others. Therefore, this charity run aims to raise funds to build a better environment for the students.
